The recipe for raspberry and blackberry smoothie

A simple smoothie taking only 10 minutes to prepare

Ingredients

  • 1 small banana
  • 1/2 cup of blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 6 ounces of vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon of Honey
  • 4 Ice cubes
  • a sprig of mint

Once you have the ingredients it's time to make the smoothie

Intructions

  1. place the banana, blackberries, yogurt, honey and ice cubes into a blender
  2. Blend until smoothie
  3. Garnish with a sprig of mint
  4. consume
