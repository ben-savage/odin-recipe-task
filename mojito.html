Recipe for making a Mojito

Another simple drinks recipe, that takes only 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 10 Fresh Mint Leaves
  • 1/2 medium lime in wedges
  • 2 Tbsp White Sugar
  • 1 1/2 ounces White rum
  • 1 cup of ice cubes
  • 1/2 Cup club soda

Instructions

  1. Place the mint leaves and one lime wedge in a glass and crush
  2. Add the remaining lime wedges and 2 Tbsp sugar and muddle
  3. Fill the glass with the ice
  4. Add the Rum and the Club Soda
  5. Stir and taste, add more sugar if desired
