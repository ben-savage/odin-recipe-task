Farppuccino Recipe

Recipe to make a simple frappuccino for two

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of ice
  • 1 Cup of strongly brewed black coffee
  • 1 cup low-fat milk
  • 1/3 cup caramel sauce
  • 3 Tbsp white sugar

Instructions

  1. Blend ice, coffee, milk, caramel sauce, and sugar together in a blender on high speed until smooth.
  2. Pour into two glasses and serve
